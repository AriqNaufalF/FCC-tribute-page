Short story about William Tanuwijaya

He was born in North Sumatera, 11 November 1981. He is an Indonesian entrepreneur who is also founder of Tokopedia, a e-commerce webstie. William who had just graduated from high school, traveled to Jakarta to get better education, following the wishes of his father and uncle. Unfortuantely in teh second year of collage, william's fahter fell ill, requirring him to find side job in order to continue studying in Jakarta. This urgent situation brought william to internet world. His job as internet cafe guard turned out to lead William to launch Tokopedia in 2009, of course after going trough various struggles that were not easy.

Tokopedia is currently one of unicorn startups The company also achieved Android Excellence Apps as Google's best app of choice on the Play Store. William was also awarded the Young Global Leaders award in 2016, as one of the world's most influential young leaders.
